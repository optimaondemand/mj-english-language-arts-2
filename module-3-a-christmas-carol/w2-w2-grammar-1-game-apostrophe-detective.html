💡 STUCK?
Read each version out loud. An apostrophe has two jobs: it shows a noun owns something (Scrooge’s voice) or it marks the missing letters in a contraction (didn’t = did not). Its with no apostrophe is a possessive pronoun. It’s with an apostrophe means “it is.”
