💡 WHAT CHANGES SCROOGE? THREE FORCES TO CONSIDER
FEAR
Ghost of Christmas Yet to Come
Scrooge sees his own name on the grave. The deserted body. The thieves laughing. No one mourns.
MEMORY
Ghost of Christmas Past
Belle’s face. Fan at the school. The young Scrooge alone. The warmth he walked away from.
LOVE
Ghost of Christmas Present
Tiny Tim’s “God bless us.” Bob’s toast. Fred’s warmth. The Cratchit dinner.
Dickens gives evidence for all three. The question is which you find most persuasive, and why.
📚 PART 1 · READ & NOTICE: BEFORE YOU READ STAVE 5
⚡ QUICK CHECK-IN
At the end of Stave 4, Scrooge is kneeling at his own grave, begging the Spirit for a chance to change. What do you expect Stave 5 to look like?
📚 PART 2 · READ & NOTICE: NOW READ STAVE 5

Stave 5 is the shortest and most joyful stave. Scrooge wakes on Christmas morning laughing and crying at the same time: “I am as light as a feather, I am as happy as an angel, I am as merry as a schoolboy.” He discovers he has not missed Christmas Day. His first act of generosity is sending the prize turkey anonymously to the Cratchit family. He donates generously to the charity collector he turned away the day before. He visits his nephew Fred for Christmas dinner and is welcomed with warmth. The next morning he arrives at the office before Bob, plays a brief joke on him, and then raises his salary and promises to help the family. Tiny Tim does not die. Dickens closes: Scrooge “became as good a friend, as good a master, and as good a man, as the good old city knew.” The final words belong to Tiny Tim: “God bless Us, Every One!”

✏️ MAKE & APPLY: WHAT DOES SCROOGE DO IN STAVE 5?
⚡ TRY IT
Use what you read to answer each question. Decide in your head first.
1. What is the first generous act Scrooge performs in Stave 5?
2. Dickens writes that Scrooge “became as good a friend, as good a master, and as good a man, as the good old city knew.” What does this sentence show about his transformation?
3. Why does Dickens end the book with Tiny Tim’s words rather than Scrooge’s?
