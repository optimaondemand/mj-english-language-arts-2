📁 SET UP YOUR DIGITAL BINDER
Start Here: Your OneDrive Course Folder
This year you will write in Microsoft Word and keep your files in OneDrive, your online binder. Set this up once and the rest of the year stays tidy. You will do this in about ten minutes.
🔑 STEP 1: SIGN IN WITH YOUR SCHOOL ACCOUNT
Go to office.com and sign in with your school Microsoft account, not a personal account. This gives you Word, PowerPoint, Excel, and OneDrive at no cost. If you cannot sign in, message your teacher; do not use a personal account for class work.
📂 STEP 2: CREATE YOUR COURSE FOLDER
In OneDrive, click New > Folder and build this structure. You only need the ELA branch for this class. The highlighted folder is the one you will use all year.
📁 OneDrive (school account)
  └─ 📁 Optima
      ├─ 📁 ELA
          └─ 📁 ELA7_Fall ◁ your course folder
      ├─ 📁 Math
      └─ 📁 History (and your other subjects)
Your teacher will tell you the exact name to use for the course folder (for example, ELA7_Fall).
🏷 STEP 3: NAME YOUR FILES THE SAME WAY EVERY TIME
Every file you make for this class follows one naming pattern. It keeps your work easy to find and easy for your teacher to grade. Here is the pattern, broken into its parts:
Your last name
LastName
Garcia
Course code
GT
GT
What it is
AssignmentName
QuickWrite1
Put together, your file name looks like this:
Garcia_GT_QuickWrite1
More examples for this module: Garcia_GT_ReadingJournal_Module1, Garcia_GT_QuickWrite5. Replace Garcia with your own last name.
📄 STEP 4: WHAT GOES IN THE FOLDER
Two kinds of file live in your course folder all module long:
●
Your Reading Journal. One Word document for the whole module. You add to it every week. It is your organizer for vocabulary, reading answers, and your project idea.
●
Your Quick Writes. One Word document each week (QuickWrite1, QuickWrite2, and so on). These are short pieces of writing that practice for your module project.
📤 STEP 5: HOW TO TURN IN A FILE
When an assignment asks for a Word document, you have two paths depending on how the assignment is set up:
A
If the assignment opens your own copy automatically: Word opens your personal copy when you open the assignment in Canvas. Do your work, then come back to Canvas and click Submit. You do not need to rename it.
B
If the assignment asks you to upload a file: Open the Canvas assignment, choose the OneDrive tab, find your correctly named file in your course folder, and submit it.
💡 GOOD TO KNOW
OneDrive saves automatically and keeps a version history, so your work is safe even if your device closes. You will not lose a Quick Write.
Joining mid-year? Make sure you can sign in to your school M365 account, build the folder above, learn the naming rule, and bookmark this page. You can find this guide linked from every module.
