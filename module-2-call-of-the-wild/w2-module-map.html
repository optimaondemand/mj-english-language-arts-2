✏️ THIS MODULE'S WRITING FOCUS
All module long you are building one argument, step by step. This week is Step 1: the thesis statement. A thesis is the one clear claim your whole argument will defend. In the Friday Workshop you will write a thesis plus one reason about how Buck is changing. Save it. You will add evidence, a counterclaim, and a conclusion in the weeks ahead, and the pieces become your final essay.
📖 ONGOING ALL MODULE
Keep your Call of the Wild Reading Journal open as you read. Add this week’s vocabulary, jot notes on Chapter 2, and write down your growing answer to the module question about what transforms Buck. Your teacher collects the Journal at the module checkpoints, not every week.
