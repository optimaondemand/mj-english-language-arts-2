✏️ YOUR WRITING THIS MODULE: BUILDING AN ARGUMENT
You are building one argument essay piece by piece. Two weeks ago you wrote a thesis. This week you add the next layer: evidence and explanation. You will learn how to back a claim with a real quote from the book and then explain why that quote proves your point. Quick Write 3 is your evidence paragraph, and it feeds straight into your final project.
