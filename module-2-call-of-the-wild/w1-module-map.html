🎯 THE MODULE QUESTION
What transforms Buck from a pampered house dog into a creature of the wild, and what does his change reveal about instinct, survival, and the pull between civilization and the wild?
This week starts the answer. Buck begins as the king of a warm California estate, and by the last page he is standing in his first snow. Watch what the new place does to him.
