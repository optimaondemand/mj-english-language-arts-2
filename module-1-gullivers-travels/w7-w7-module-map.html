🎯 THIS WEEK'S QUESTION
Gulliver is sent away from the land of the wise horses and returns to England a different man. He cannot stand the sight of his own family. How did the voyage change him, and what is Swift saying about pride by the time the book ends?

📝 THE PROJECT OPENS THIS WEEK
This week your Module 1 Final Project opens. It has two parts, and they live in Canvas in the Module Wrappers and Project area:
GT Final Project — Written component: a short argument answering which voyage best reveals Swift’s view of humanity. You build it from your Quick Writes, including this week’s Quick Write 7.
GT Final Project — Creative and VR component: a visual contrast of Lilliput and Brobdingnag plus an immersive VR scene staging the scale contrast. You plan that scene this week in VR Activity 2: Project Storyboard.
Open both project pages now and read what they ask. Then keep this week’s Quick Write 7 and your saved VR storyboard. They feed straight into the project, which is due in Week 8.
📖 ONGOING ALL MODULE
Keep your Gulliver’s Travels Reading Journal open as you read Chapters 9 through 12. Add this week’s vocabulary, note the early hint that trouble is coming for Gulliver, and record how he behaves once he is back in England. Your teacher collects the Journal at the module checkpoints, not every week.
