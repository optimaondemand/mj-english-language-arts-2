📤 WHAT IS DUE THIS WEEK
Both parts of the Module 1 Final Project are due by the end of Week 8. The two deliverable pages live in Canvas in the Module Wrappers and Project area. Open each one and follow its directions:
GT Final Project — Written: your short argument (claim, two pieces of evidence with explanation, and a conclusion) on which voyage best reveals Swift’s view of humanity. Submitted as a Word document.
GT Final Project — Creative + VR: your visual contrast of Lilliput and Brobdingnag (diorama, digital art, or comic strip), your VR scale-contrast scene as a video or screenshot, and your 200 to 250 word reflection.
Both components are scored with the same rubric: GT Final Project — Rubric. Read it before you submit so you know what earns full credit.
