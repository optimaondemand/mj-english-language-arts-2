🎯 THIS WEEK'S QUESTION
Last voyage Gulliver towered over Lilliput. Now he is the size of their thumb. When you go from large to small, what does the change reveal about pride, in others and in yourself?
🔄 THE BIG SHIFT THIS WEEK
WEEKS 1–3 · LILLIPUT
Gulliver = the giant
He looks down on a tiny, proud nation.
➞
WEEK 4 · BROBDINGNAG
Gulliver = the tiny one
Now giants look down on him, and the satire turns on us.
